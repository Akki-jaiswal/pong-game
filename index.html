🎮 How to Play

  • Use ↑ and ↓ keys or touch to move your paddle.
  • First to score 3 points and lead by 2 wins the game.
  • Select difficulty: Easy, Medium, or Hard.
  • Use Pause/Resume to control the game state.
  • Use Restart to reset the current game and start over.
